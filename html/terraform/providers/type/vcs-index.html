Version Control Providers

This is a group of Version Control System (VCS) providers that offer capabilities of using Terraform to manage your VCS projects, teams and repositories. Terraform integrates with these services to create and manage resources provided by the VCS. These providers are primarily supported by the vendor in close collaboration with HashiCorp, and are tested by HashiCorp.
