tensorflow::ops::QuantizeAndDequantizeV4Grad

#include <array_ops.h>

Returns the gradient of QuantizeAndDequantizeV4.

Summary

Returns a gradient of 1 for inputs that are within the quantization range, or 0 otherwise.
