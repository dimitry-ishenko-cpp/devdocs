tensorflow::ops::Log

#include <math_ops.h>

Computes natural logarithm of x element-wise.

Summary

I.e., \(y = \log_e x\).

Example:

x = tf.constant([0, 0.5, 1, 5])
tf.math.log(x) ==> [-inf, -0.6931472,  0. ,  1.609438]

Args:

  • scope: A Scope object

Returns:

  • Output: The y tensor.
