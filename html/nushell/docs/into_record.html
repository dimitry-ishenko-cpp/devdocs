into record for conversions

Convert value to record.

Signature

> into record {flags}

Input/output types:

input output
datetime record
duration record
list<any> record
range record
record record
table record

Examples

Convert from one row table to record

> [[value]; [false]] |into record
╭───────┬───────╮
│value│false│
╰───────┴───────╯

Convert from list to record

> [123] |into record
╭───┬───╮
│0│1│
│1│2│
│2│3│
╰───┴───╯

Convert from range to record

>0..2|into record
╭───┬───╮
│0│0│
│1│1│
│2│2│
╰───┴───╯

convert duration to record (weeks max)

> (-500day-4hr-5sec) |into record
╭────────┬────╮
│week│71│
│day│3│
│hour│4│
│second│5│
│sign│-│
╰────────┴────╯

convert record to record

> {a:1, b:2} |into record
╭───┬───╮
│a│1│
│b│2│
╰───┴───╯

convert date to record

>2020-04-12T22:10:57+02:00|into record
╭──────────┬────────╮
│year│2020│
│month│4│
│day│12│
│hour│22│
│minute│10│
│second│57│
│timezone│+02:00│
╰──────────┴────────╯
