Teams

[edit on GitHub]

Overview

A Chef Automate team is an assigned grouping of users. You can import existing teams into Chef Automate with Microsoft AD (LDAP), generic LDAP, or SAML. You can also create local Chef Automate teams that are independent of LDAP or SAML.

Permission for the iam:teams action is required to interact with teams. Any user that is part of the admins team or the Administrator policy will have this permission. Otherwise, IAM custom policies can be created to assign this permission.

Managing Local Teams

Admins Team

Chef Automate comes with an Admins team by default. Local users can be added directly to this team, which assigns admin permissions to users.

Creating Local Teams

Adding Local Users to Teams

Removing Local Users from Teams

To remove local users from a team, navigate to Teams from the Settings tab and locate the team. Navigate to the teamâ€™s page, locate the user to remove, and then use the menu at the end of the table row to remove the user.

Changing Team Details

Teams have both a team name and the projects that a team belongs to. To change, navigate to Teams from the Settings tab, select an individual team, and then navigate to the Details tab.

Deleting Local Teams

Navigate to Teams in the Settings tab. Then open the menu at the end of the table row and select Delete Team.
