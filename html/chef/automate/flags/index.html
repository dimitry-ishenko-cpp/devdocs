Feature Flags

[edit on GitHub]

Use the beta, lega, or feat flags to turn on or off beta, legacy, or in-development features.

Select anywhere on the Chef Automate interface and enter “feat”, “lega”, or “beta” to open the feature flags window. Toggle your desired feature to either the “ON” or “OFF” position.
