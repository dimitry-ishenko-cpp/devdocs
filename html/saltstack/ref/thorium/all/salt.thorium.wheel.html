salt.thorium.wheel

React by calling asynchronous runners

salt.thorium.wheel.cmd(name, fun=None, arg=(), **kwargs)

Execute a runner asynchronous:

USAGE:

run_cloud:
  wheel.cmd:
    - fun: key.delete
    - match: minion_id
