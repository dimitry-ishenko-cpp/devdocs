salt.engines.reactor

Setup Reactor

Example Config in Master or Minion config

engines:
  - reactor:
      refresh_interval: 60
      worker_threads: 10
      worker_hwm: 10000

reactor:
  - 'salt/cloud/*/destroyed':
    - /srv/reactor/destroy/*.sls

salt.engines.reactor.start(refresh_interval=None, worker_threads=None, worker_hwm=None)
