salt.states.ntp

Management of NTP servers

New in version 2014.1.0.

This state is used to manage NTP servers. Currently only Windows is supported.

win_ntp:
  ntp.managed:
    - servers:
      - pool.ntp.org
      - us.pool.ntp.org

salt.states.ntp.managed(name, servers=None)

Manage NTP servers

servers

A list of NTP servers
