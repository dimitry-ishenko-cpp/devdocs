class URI::WSS

Parent:
URI::WS

The default port for WSS URIs is 443, and the scheme is ‘wss:’ rather than ‘ws:’. Other than that, WSS URIs are identical to WS URIs; see URI::WS.

Constants

DEFAULT_PORT

A Default port of 443 for URI::WSS
