tf.keras.backend.experimental.enable_tf_random_generator

Enable the tf.random.Generator as the RNG for Keras.

tf.keras.backend.experimental.enable_tf_random_generator()

See tf.keras.backend.experimental.is_tf_random_generator_enabled for more details.
