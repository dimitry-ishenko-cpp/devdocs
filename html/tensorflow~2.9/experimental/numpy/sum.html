tf.experimental.numpy.sum

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's sum.

tf.experimental.numpy.sum(
    a, axis=None, dtype=None, keepdims=None
)

Unsupported arguments: out, initial, where.

See the NumPy documentation for numpy.sum.
