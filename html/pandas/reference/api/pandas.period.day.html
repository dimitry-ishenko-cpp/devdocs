pandas.Period.day

Period.day

Get day of the month that a Period falls on.

Returns:
int

See also

Period.dayofweek

Get the day of the week.

Period.dayofyear

Get the day of the year.

Examples

>>> p = pd.Period("2018-03-11", freq='h')
>>> p.day
11
