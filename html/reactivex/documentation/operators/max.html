In RxJava, this operator is not in the ReactiveX core, but is part of the distinct rxjava-math module.

RxJS max maxBy

max and maxBy are found in the following distributions:

  • rx.all.js
  • rx.all.compat.js
  • rx.aggregates.js

They requires one of the following:

  • rx.js
  • rx.compat.js
  • rx.lite.js
  • rx.lite.compat.js
