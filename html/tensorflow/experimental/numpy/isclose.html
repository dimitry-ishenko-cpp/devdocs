tf.experimental.numpy.isclose

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's isclose.

tf.experimental.numpy.isclose(
    a, b, rtol=1e-05, atol=1e-08, equal_nan=False
)

See the NumPy documentation for numpy.isclose.
