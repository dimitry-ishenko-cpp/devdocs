tf.experimental.numpy.conjugate

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's conjugate.

tf.experimental.numpy.conjugate(
    x
)

Unsupported arguments: out, where, casting, order, dtype, subok, signature, extobj.

See the NumPy documentation for numpy.conjugate.
