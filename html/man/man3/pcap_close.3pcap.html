NAME 

       pcap_close - close a capture device or savefile

SYNOPSIS 

       #include <pcap/pcap.h>

       void pcap_close(pcap_t *p);

DESCRIPTION 

       pcap_close() closes the files associated with p and deallocates
       resources.

SEE ALSO 

       pcap(3PCAP)
