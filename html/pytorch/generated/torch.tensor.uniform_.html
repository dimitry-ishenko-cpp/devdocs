torch.Tensor.uniform_

Tensor.uniform_(from=0, to=1, *, generator=None) → Tensor

Fills self tensor with numbers sampled from the continuous uniform distribution:

P(x)=1to−fromP(x) = \dfrac{1}{\text{to} - \text{from}}
