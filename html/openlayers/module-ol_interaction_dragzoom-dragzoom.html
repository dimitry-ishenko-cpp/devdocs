ol/interaction/DragZoom~DragZoom 


import DragZoom from 'ol/interaction/DragZoom.js';

Allows the user to zoom the map by clicking and dragging on the map, normally combined with a ol/events/condition that limits it to when a key, shift by default, is held down.

To change the style of the box, use CSS and the .ol-dragzoom selector, or your custom one configured with className.
