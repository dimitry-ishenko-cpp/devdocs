ol/layer/Group.GroupEvent


A layer group triggers 'addlayer' and 'removelayer' events when layers are added to or removed from the group or one of its child groups. When a layer group is added to or removed from another layer group, a single event will be triggered (instead of one per layer in the group added or removed).
