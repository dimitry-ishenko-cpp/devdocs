30 Geometry

Much of the geometry code in Octave is based on the Qhull library12. Some of the documentation for Qhull, particularly for the options that can be passed to delaunay, voronoi and convhull, etc., is relevant to Octave users.

  • Voronoi Diagrams
  • Convex Hull
  • Interpolation on Scattered Data
  • Vector Rotation Matrices
