mpl_toolkits.mplot3d.art3d.line_2d_to_3d

mpl_toolkits.mplot3d.art3d.line_2d_to_3d(line, zs=0, zdir='z')[source]

Convert a Line2D to a Line3D object.

Parameters:
zsfloat

The location along the zdir axis in 3D space to position the line.

zdir{'x', 'y', 'z'}

Plane to plot line orthogonal to. Default: 'z'. See get_dir_vector for a description of the values.
