37 Packages

Since Octave is Free Software users are encouraged to share their programs with others. To aid this sharing Octave supports the installation of extra packages. The ‘Octave Forge’ project is a community-maintained set of packages that can be downloaded and installed in Octave. At the time of writing the ‘Octave Forge’ project can be found online at https://octave.sourceforge.io, but since the Internet is an ever-changing place this may not be true at the time of reading. Therefore it is recommended to see the Octave websitefor an updated reference.
