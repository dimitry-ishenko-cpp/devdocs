Construct an iterator.

1) Default constructor. Value-initializes the underlying iterator to Base and the iterator to PatternBase, and initializes the pointer to parent join_with_view with nullptr.
2) Conversion from /*iterator*/<false> to /*iterator*/<true>. Move constructs corresponding members.

This iterator also has a private constructor which is used by join_with_view::begin and join_with_view::end. This constructor is not accessible to users.
