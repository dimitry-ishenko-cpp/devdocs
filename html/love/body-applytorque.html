Body:applyTorque

Apply torque to a body.

Torque is like a force that will change the angular velocity (spin) of a body. The effect will depend on the rotational inertia a body has.

Function

Synopsis

Body:applyTorque( torque )

Arguments

number torque
The torque to apply.

Returns

Nothing.
