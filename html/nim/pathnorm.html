Example:

when defined(posix):
  doAssert normalizePath("./foo//bar/../baz") == "foo/baz"
  • Turns multiple slashes into single slashes.
  • Resolves '/foo/../bar' to '/bar'.
  • Removes './' from the path, but "foo/.." becomes ".".
