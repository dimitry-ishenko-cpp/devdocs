Construct an iterator.

1) Default constructor. Value-initializes the parent_ with nullptr and default-initializes the current_.
2) Conversion from /*iterator*/<false> to /*iterator*/<true>. Initializes parent_ with i.parent_ and current_ with std::move(i.current_).
3) A private constructor which is used by cartesian_product_view::begin and cartesian_product_view::end. This constructor is not accessible to users. Initializes parent_ with std::addressof(parent) and current_ with std::move(current).
