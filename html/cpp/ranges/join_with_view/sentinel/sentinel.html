Constructs a sentinel.

1) Default constructor. Value-initializes the underlying sentinel.
2) Conversion from /*sentinel*/<false> to /*sentinel*/<true>. Move constructs the underlying sentinel with the corresponding member of i.

This type also has a private constructor which is used by join_with_view::end. This constructor is not accessible to users.
