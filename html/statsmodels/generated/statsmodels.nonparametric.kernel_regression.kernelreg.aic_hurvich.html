statsmodels.nonparametric.kernel_regression.KernelReg.aic_hurvich

KernelReg.aic_hurvich(bw, func=None) [source]

Computes the AIC Hurvich criteria for the estimation of the bandwidth.

Parameters: bw (str or array_like) – See the bw parameter of KernelReg for details.
Returns:
  • aic (ndarray) – The AIC Hurvich criteria, one element for each variable.
  • func (None) – Unused here, needed in signature because it’s used in cv_loo.
