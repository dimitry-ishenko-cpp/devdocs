MBR Definition

Description

The MBR (Minimum Bounding Rectangle), or Envelope is the bounding geometry, formed by the minimum and maximum (X,Y) coordinates:

Examples

((MINX MINY, MAXX MINY, MAXX MAXY, MINX MAXY, MINX MINY))
