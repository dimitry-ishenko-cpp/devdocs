&

Syntax

&

Description

Bitwise AND. Converts the values to binary and compares bits. Only if both the corresponding bits are 1 is the resulting bit also 1.

See also bitwise OR.

Examples

SELECT 2&1;
+-----+
| 2&1 |
+-----+
|   0 |
+-----+

SELECT 3&1;
+-----+
| 3&1 |
+-----+
|   1 |
+-----+

SELECT 29 & 15;
+---------+
| 29 & 15 |
+---------+
|      13 |
+---------+
