\linethickness

Synopsis: 

\linethickness{dim}

Declares the thickness of subsequent horizontal and vertical lines in a picture to be dim, which must be a positive length (see Lengths). It differs from \thinlines and \thicklines in that it does not affect the thickness of slanted lines, circles, or ovals (see \oval).
